<Update label="January 2026" tags={["Python"]} description="v0.0.0">
  ### Current Endpoints

  - `ohlc_forecast` analyses the causal structure of a financial time series to identify either the initiation of a new causal trajectory or the persistence of an existing one.
  - `univariate_forecast` analyses the causal structure of a univariate time series to identify either the initiation of a new causal trajectory or the persistence of an existing one.

  ### New Endpoints

  - `ohlc_forecast_v2` analyses the causal structure of a financial time series to identify either the initiation of a new causal trajectory or the persistence of an existing one with no reliance on timestamps.
  - `unvariate_forecast_v2` analyses the causal structure of a univariate time series to identify either the initiation of a new causal trajectory or the persistence of an existing one with no reliance on timestamps.
  - `rmsf_forecast` analyses the causal structure of root mean square fluctuation values to identify either the initiation of a new causal trajectory or the persistence of an existing one with no reliance on timestamps.
